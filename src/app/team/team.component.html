Dear Reader,
Delhi Technological University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering) is one of the premier institutes of the nation and needs no introduction. Established almost 7 decades ago, the institute is widely acclaimed for its eminence in the field of engineering education. Instituted with an aim to cater to the needs of Indian industries for prowess manpower, DTU has achieved manifold milestones and its journey uphill continue. It has played a pivotal role in the reconstruction, modernization and administration of the society by producing exceptional and meritorious students year after year. The student community of DTU been internationally admired in recent times by getting numerous accolades overseas in events like formula racing car, super mileage vehicle, all-terrain vehicle, unmanned aerial vehicle, underwater vehicle, hybrid vehicle, solar car etc.

In its bid to nurture innovative and creative abilities and foster enterprising spirit amongst students from all over the country, INNOVA DTU continues to create legacy together. During INNOVA, DTU witnesses colossal participation from technical institutions and some of the most prodigious brains in the country possessing great intellect, caliber and acumen in domains ranging from engineering to entrepreneurship. An event of this magnitude provides a platform for an organization of your repute to develop synergetic relations with the students by presenting your case studies, workshops, industry- oriented problems and regale itself to publicity in the form of posters, banners and media coverage. We invite you to be a part of this techno-managerial extravaganza by virtue of your association/sponsorship with us.
